## 1. Process routes

Copper tube is made by three main routes. The relative share of each has shifted heavily toward cast-and-roll since the 1990s, particularly for ACR (air-conditioning and refrigeration) tube.

### 1.1 Extrusion + drawing (classic / legacy)

Cathode → shaft/reverberatory melt → semi-continuous or continuous cast log or tube round → cut to billets → reheat to ~835 °C → pierce → extrude through die over mandrel (mother tube, typically ~70 mm OD × ~26 m) → cool & clean → pilger or cold-draw in multiple passes over floating plug or fixed mandrel → intermediate anneal → finish draw → final anneal → cut / coil → ECT → pack.

- Copper Development Association — *How Do They Do That? Making Copper Plumbing Tube* — https://www.copper.org/publications/newsletters/innovations/1998/09/howdo_tube.php
- Copper Development Association — *Cu/Alloy Tube and Pipe overview* — https://copper.org/applications/plumbing/overview/cu_alloy_tube_pipe.php
- OneMonroe — *How Copper Pipes Are Manufactured* — https://monroeengineering.com/blog/how-copper-pipes-are-manufactured/
- Multimet — *How copper pipes are made* — https://www.multimetcopper.com/how-copper-pipes-are-made-a-behind-the-scenes-look-at-the-manufacturing-process/
- Design Life-Cycle — *Copper Tubing* — http://www.designlife-cycle.com/copper-tubing
- ChinaTiAlloy — *Copper Tube Manufacturing Process* — https://www.chinatialloy.com/info/copper-tube-manufacturing-process-103089610.html
- Fabricator — *Cold drawing principles* — https://www.thefabricator.com/tubepipejournal/article/tubepipeproduction/cold-drawing-principles

### 1.2 Cast-and-Roll / Planetary rolling / directube® (modern)

Horizontal continuous casting of a thick-walled hollow (mother tube) directly from cathodes → milling of outer surface → planetary rolling to reduce OD and wall → combined drawing → spinner block drawing → LWC winder → anneal.

- SMS group — *directube®* copper tube plants — https://www.sms-group.com/plants/copper-tube-lines
- Ruby Copper / Toan Phat — end-to-end process description (continuous casting → combined drawing → spinner block → LWC/pancake) — https://www.rubycopper.vn/production-process.html
- Entrepreneur India DPR — *Inner Grooved Copper Tube* (describes Outokumpu "Cast and Roll" technology from Pori, Finland, 1987) — https://entrepreneurindia.co/public/index.php/project-details/inner-grooved-copper-tube-manufacturing-plant-detailed-project-report
- Hailiang 5th-generation intelligent cast-and-roll line (yield 91→93 %, energy −300 kWh/t, labour ×3) — https://news.metal.com/newscontent/101549361/

### 1.3 Up-cast (upward continuous casting) — oxygen-free copper

Molten copper drawn vertically under charcoal / flake-graphite / O₂ isolation. Gives OF-OK® style oxygen-free copper (<10 ppm O₂) for electrical and electronic tubing.

- CCM Copper — *Up-drawing method* explanation — https://ccmcoppermouldtube.com/copper-tube-production-process/
- Luvata — *Tubes* and OF-OK® range — https://luvata.com/products/tubes/

### 1.4 Cold pilger rolling

Reciprocating ring-die + tapered mandrel process giving up to ~90 % area reduction for copper in one pass, better dimensional control than drawing, no cleaning/annealing between steps.

- Fabricator — *Introducing cold pilger mill technology* — https://www.thefabricator.com/tubepipejournal/article/tubepipeproduction/introducing-cold-pilger-mill-technology
- SMS group cold pilger mill — https://www.sms-group.com/plants/cold-pilger-mill
- Total Materia — *Cold pilger rolling* — https://www.totalmateria.com/en-us/articles/cold-pilger-rolling-1/
- Pilger Mill Technologies — http://pilgermilltechnologies.com/What_is_Pilgering.html

### 1.5 Inner-grooving (for enhanced heat-transfer tube)

Plain LWC drawn through a grooving head while a small floating ball inside the tube forms the internal ribs.

- SMS VNVG 2000 inner-grooving line (head up to 49 000 rpm, line speed up to 100 m/min) — https://www.sms-group.com/plants/copper-tube-lines
- Golden Dragon inner-grooved tube product description — https://www.gdcobre.com/copper.html
- Fabmann — https://www.fabmann.com/copper-products/groove-copper-tube/inner-grooved-copper-tube.html
