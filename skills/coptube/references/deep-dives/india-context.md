# Deep Dive — India Context

The council cites this file when a user is in India or asks about Indian producers, incentives, clusters, and local economics. Numbers are from Government of India (PIB), company disclosures, DPRs, and trade press.

---

## 1. Why copper tube is a strategic product for India right now

- Air-conditioner penetration is still under 10 % of Indian households, against 90 %+ in developed markets, so the domestic AC market is on a multi-decade growth curve.
- Renewable energy (solar + wind + grid) and EV charging drive electrical-grade Cu demand.
- India imports a material share of ACR-grade inner-grooved copper tube (IGT) today; import substitution is a stated national priority.
- Government has named plain and grooved copper tube explicitly as "high-value AC intermediate" in the PLI White Goods scheme.

## 2. PLI Scheme for White Goods (AC & LED)

- **Outlay:** ₹6,238 crore.
- **Duration:** FY 2021-22 to FY 2028-29.
- **Approved cabinet:** 7 April 2021; notified by DPIIT 16 April 2021.
- **Domestic value-add target:** raise from 20–25 % to 75–80 % by FY 2028-29.
- **Covered AC intermediates (high-value):** compressors (including oil-free and high-capacity), **copper tubes (plain and/or grooved)**, aluminium stock for foils / fins for heat exchangers.
- **Covered AC intermediates (low-value):** control assemblies (IDU/ODU/remotes), display panels, motors, CFF, valves and brass components, heat exchangers, sheet-metal, plastic-moulded components.
- **Cumulative status (through early 2025, per PIB):** 84 companies selected, committed investment ~₹10,478 crore; third round alone: 24 beneficiaries, committed investment ~₹3,516 crore; ~₹281.4 crore incentive disbursed through early 2025.
- **Geographic distribution:** 13 states, 49 locations, 54 districts, 18 states overall; clusters at Noida/Greater Noida (UP), Neemrana/Bhiwari (Rajasthan), Aurangabad/Pune (Maharashtra), Sanand (Gujarat), Sri City (Andhra Pradesh — "the Cooling City", 6 AC manufacturers + 12 component manufacturers).
- **Fourth-round application window:** re-opened 15 Sep 2025, extended to 10 Nov 2025 per DPIIT.

Named AC component beneficiaries (not exhaustive): Daikin, Voltas, Hindalco, Amber, PG Technoplast, Epack, Mettube, LG, Blue Star, Johnson Hitachi, Panasonic, Haier, Midea, Havells, IFB, Nidec, Lucas, Swaminathan, Triton Valves.

## 3. Broader policy stack supporting a new tube plant in India

- **Make in India** — umbrella policy, capex subsidy co-participation with states.
- **Atmanirbhar Bharat** — self-reliance framing.
- **State industrial policies** — Gujarat (IIDB), Maharashtra (MIDC), Karnataka (KIADB), Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu. Typical incentives: stamp-duty exemption, power-tariff rebate for 5–7 years, capital subsidy 15–25 % on fixed investment up to a cap, SGST reimbursement.
- **SEZ / NIMZ** — for export-oriented plants.
- **BIS requirements** — ISI marking for plumbing and heat-exchanger tube applies in the domestic market.

## 4. Indian producers — deeper map

### 4.1 Hindustan Copper Ltd. (HCL)
- Only vertically integrated copper producer in India (mining → smelting → refining → value add).
- Central public-sector enterprise under Ministry of Mines.
- Sources: https://www.hindustancopper.com/Home ; https://mines.gov.in/webportal/content/hcl

### 4.2 Hindalco Industries (Aditya Birla Group)
- Birla Copper smelter at Dahej, Gujarat — one of the world's largest single-location custom smelters (~500 kt/yr cathodes).
- New **Inner-Grooved Copper Tube (IGT) plant at Waghodia, Gujarat** — India's first backward-integrated IGT plant.
  - **Phase 1:** 25 000 tpa.
  - **Phase 2:** planned expansion to 50 000 tpa.
  - Plus **11 500 tpa battery-grade copper foil** on the same complex.
- Uses Hindalco internal cathode feedstock, addressing the ACR import-substitution story head-on.
- Sources: https://tubepipeindia.com/hindalco-to-establish-indias-first/ ; Hindalco annual reports — https://www.hindalco.com/upload/pdf/Hindalco_Annual_Report_FY2006.pdf

### 4.3 Mehta Tubes / Mexflow (Mehta Group)
- Valsad, Gujarat. ISO 9001:2015. Plumbing, medical gas, ACR, specialty copper tubes. Brand "Mexflow" for plumbing & HVAC.
- Group network: Metal Gems and allied trading companies.
- Sources: https://www.mehtatubes.com/ ; https://www.mexflow.com/ ; https://www.mehta-group.com/

### 4.4 MetTube (Mettube Berhad, Malaysia) — strongly present in India market
- Shah Alam, Malaysia, global Cu tube major, both extrusion-and-draw and cast-and-roll routes.
- Product range: LWC (levelled-wound coil), pancake, straight-length, inner-grooved (OD 5.0–12.7 mm, wall 0.24–0.55 mm).
- Major India distributors: Kunj International, Manibhadra Fittings, Kalirajimpex.
- India beneficiary under PLI White Goods (for the local AC ecosystem).
- Sources: https://mettube.com/ ; https://mettube.com/copper-tubes/

## 5. Clusters and logistics

- **Gujarat** — Dahej, Sanand, Waghodia, Mundra/Kandla ports. Metal-heavy cluster, power-reliable, Hindalco anchor, PLI-attractive.
- **Maharashtra** — Aurangabad-Pune corridor; Nashik; MIDC infrastructure, deep auto/AC OEM ecosystem.
- **Tamil Nadu / Sri City (AP)** — "Cooling City"; Daikin, Isuzu, Foxconn-adjacent supplier base.
- **Karnataka** — Bengaluru-Tumakuru corridor; EV, AC, electronics ecosystem; Triton Valves presence.
- **UP** — Noida/Greater Noida electronics & white-goods cluster.
- **Rajasthan** — Neemrana/Bhiwari, Japanese industrial zone; AC producers nearby.

## 6. Indian tube plant — realistic cost bands (2025/26 money)

- **Copper raw material dominance:** cathode price (LME + premium) drives 85–90 % of OpEx. July 2025 India cathode/Cu-tube raw prices around ₹900+/kg (industrytoday.co.uk reference).
- **Plant CAPEX orders of magnitude** (planning numbers; always verify with a DPR):
  - 12 000 tpa ACR IGT plant: ~USD 15 million plant & machinery (Entrepreneur India / Outokumpu Cast-and-Roll reference case).
  - 20 000–40 000 tpa plant: "significant capital across land, civil works, extrusion presses, drawing benches, annealing furnaces" — see India Plant Setup 2026 guide.
- **Illustrative profitability from the same DPR:** net profit ~USD 3.85 million at 12 000 tpa; ~USD 8.75 million at 20 000 tpa — subject to LME settlement.
- **Gross margin range in India:** 15–25 %; net margin 5–10 % (India Plant Setup 2026 guide).
- **Payback:** depends heavily on product mix (plumbing vs. ACR IGT vs. medical gas) and capacity utilisation.

## 7. Standards, certifications and testing infrastructure in India

- BIS IS 191, IS 407, IS 1545, IS 2501, IS 2575 — Cu and Cu-alloy tube and sheet.
- ASTM / EN / JIS equivalents accepted for export.
- Third-party NDT service providers: Intertek, TÜV-India, SGS, Bureau Veritas, UL.
- Testing labs with ECT / UT capability: National Test House, CSIR-NML, and many accredited private labs.

## 8. Practical advice the council gives an Indian user

- For a greenfield tube plant in 2026, short-list SMS group (Germany) and a Chinese turn-key partner (e.g., Senbo / TED) for capex versus performance trade-off. Pair with a Level 2 from the line supplier plus an independent MES (Ignition-based or PSI Metals for the bigger plant).
- Use the PLI White Goods scheme for copper tube as a high-value AC intermediate — time applications with the DPIIT window (Round 4 extended into Nov 2025).
- Co-locate with an AC or HVAC OEM cluster (Sanand / Sri City / Aurangabad / Neemrana) for captive offtake and logistics efficiency.
- Budget 10–15 % of capex as automation and IT / MES / ERP integration — it is the difference between Hailiang-class yield (~93 %) and Indian-average legacy (~87 %).
- Invest in operator training on modern HMIs and Level 2 concepts; the skills gap is the biggest hidden risk.
